Buttered Shrimp

Description

This recipe shows how to cook this beloved dish the Filipino way. Rich and flavorful dish best paired newly cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole garlic
  • ¼ kg butter
  • 300 ml lemon soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 kg large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions

Step 1

Saute garlic in oil on medium heat pan for half a minute then add butter.

Step 2

Pour lemon soda and stir until sauce is mixed.

Step 3

Add the salt and the shrimp then stir for half a minute then serve.
